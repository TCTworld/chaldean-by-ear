Main Source of Information:

Introductory Chaldean by Bp. Sarhad Y. Jammo and Fr. Andrew Younan

This book is an introduction to the Chaldean-Aramaic language, with exercises in alphabet and vowels, basic conversation, common phrases, introductory vocabulary, reading exercises, literature samples, and a 2000 word glossary. The accuracy of the book's translation of Chaldean-Aramaric to English is validated by my parents, who are fluent speakers of the niche language portrayed book and confirmed that the translations and pronounciations are accurate. This is one of the very few tools out there to truly learn the language, as there is not much educational documentation related to the obscurity of the Chaldean language.
